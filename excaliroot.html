About the project

Excaliroot

A game about roots and capybaras made in Unreal Engine 4

This videogame was made during the Global Game Jam 2023. It took a weekend, some sleep deprivation and the brains of 6 people to complete.

In Excaliroot the player will embodie a capybara, the king of rodents, and he will need to fight against the enemies that want to dethrone him, the porcupines. To do so, he will need to find the sacred carrot sword and take it out from the ground.

Some of the things that I worked on for this project were related to the gameplay and the UI, like the health and hits system, the timer or the porcupines spawners.
