About the project

Puddies

A casual platform, puzzle and crowd management game made in Unreal Engine 4 and C++

This videogame was made in collaboration with more than 30 people from different areas of knowledge as part of my master's degree final project.

During the development of the game, my roles were mainly Gameplay, AI and UI programmer. I was in charge of the implementation of different mechanics of the player and creatures (puddies), as well as several screens to provide visual feedback to the player.
