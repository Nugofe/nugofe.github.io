About the project

Shuvani

An isometric rogelike game made in Unreal Engine 4 and C++

This game was made in collaboration with more than 30 people from different areas of knowledge. It was initially started as another group's final project of my master degree.

My roles during the development of this game are related to QA, Gameplay, AI and performance improvement. I was in charge of fixing bugs related to different elements of the game, the implementation of a new type of enemy (the enemy bomber), the improvement of enemy spawns and the improvement of game performance (reducing innecesary draw calls and base pass instructions, analyze whether the VFX are sufficiently well optimized etc).

This game has participated in the Dreamhack Valencia 2023, CometCon Gijón 2023 and IndieDevDay Barcelona 2023, and it is a contestant in the PlayStation Talents 2023 and Indie Games Málaga 2023.
